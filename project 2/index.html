Our Specializations

General Physician (Internal Medicine)

Diagnoses common illnesses like infections, diabetes, and hypertension.

Cardiologist

Specializes in heart-related conditions such as heart attacks and arrhythmias.

Neurologist

Deals with disorders of the brain and nervous system like epilepsy and migraines.

Ophthalmologist (Eye Specialist)

Treats eye conditions such as cataracts and glaucoma.

Dermatologist

Specializes in skin conditions like acne and eczema.

Orthopedic Surgeon

Focuses on bones, joints, and muscle conditions like fractures and arthritis.
